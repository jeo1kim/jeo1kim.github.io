Smart Travel

Our idea is to develop a pocket-sized travel companion, Routie, that is tailored to the customer's personal vacation. Routie’s learning and guidance algorithm is there to perfect the customer's pre-travel plans and efficiently guide them through their trip. Routie will be the only service that users need to feel safe and accomplished on their vacation.

People love to travel, but planning a perfect trip takes a lot of effort. It’s very hard and complicated to plan a trip to a country that you’ve never visited before. How do you pick and choose the right activities for you? How do you learn about the locations that you’ll find interesting and would want to visit? Most importantly, how will you organize your day and how will you figure out how to travel from one place to another once you’re there?

Routie uses the following technology to solve this

  • Yelp, Parse, Bing, Google Location & Maps, Facebook API.
  • Recommendation Algorithm.
  • Routing Algorithm.
  • Custom Tinder UI.
